# SynthFin Trading Bench: Corpus (v1, 1000 × 10)

**A contamination-free, fully synthetic market corpus for benchmarking LLM trading agents.**
This dataset is the frozen evaluation corpus for [SynthFin Trading
Bench](https://github.com/David-Hedgefund/synthfin-trading-bench). The evaluation harness, scorer,
baselines, and leaderboard live in that repository (Apache-2.0). **This corpus is proprietary
and gated**. See *Access & license* below.

> **Access is manual and evaluation-only.** Approval does **not** grant redistribution rights or
> permission to use the data for model training. See
> [SynthFin Evaluation License v1.0](https://github.com/David-Hedgefund/synthfin-trading-bench/blob/main/docs/CORPUS_LICENSE.md).

## Why it exists

Real-market trading benchmarks leak into training data over time. This corpus is generated by
the SynthFin engine and has **never been published**, so no model can have memorized it:
contamination-free *by construction*. Keeping the corpus gated (rather than public) is what
preserves that guarantee release-over-release.

## What's inside

- **~1,000 operating tickers × 10 macro regimes** (soft landing, AI-bubble deflation, credit
  stress, energy shock, geopolitical shock, …), ~1.6 years each.
- Daily price paths, quarterly fundamentals, earnings, guidance, analyst estimates, corporate
  actions, 13F/insider panels, and dated unstructured docs (news, 10-K/10-Q MD&A, risk factors,
  8-Ks, transcripts), every field carrying its own point-in-time availability date.
- Standard SynthFin export layout (`index.json` + `structured/` + `unstructured/`). Full field
  reference: [`docs/DATA_CARD.md`](https://github.com/David-Hedgefund/synthfin-trading-bench/blob/main/docs/DATA_CARD.md).

## Intended use & limitations

Benchmarking sequential trading agents in a contamination-free setting; information-access
ablations; regime-conditioned analysis. **Not** a source of real-market facts and **not** for
training. Tickers reuse real symbols as labels only; the data is invented and does not describe
the real companies. No PII; all documents are machine-generated.
